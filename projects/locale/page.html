Case Study

Objective

Create a seamless platform designed to empower users to discover and engage with authentic local experiences, moving beyond conventional tourist offerings.

Process

My design process for Locale focused on an iterative approach. I began by developing a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) based on an initial prototype provided by a befriended UX Designer. This MVP underwent rigorous testing with 12 target users, and their invaluable feedback directly informed subsequent design refinements, culminating in the final product.

Outcome

Locale's development helped sharpen my skills in user-centered design, particularly in translating complex user needs into intuitive and engaging mobile experiences. The project provided valuable insights into building platforms that foster community and deliver authentic value.

Standout Features

  • Detailed Experience Pages

  • Community Contributions
